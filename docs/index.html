IONIA / MONTCALM COURT RECORD INTAKE

This page is for individuals who believe they experienced procedural misconduct, record irregularities, or unfair treatment in Ionia County or Montcalm County Circuit Court.

You do NOT need to be a lawyer.
You do NOT need to write your life story.
We are collecting specific documents to identify repeat patterns.

IMPORTANT RULES

  • Upload copies — never originals
  • PDFs preferred (photos/screenshots OK)
  • Do not alter documents
  • Only upload items that match the categories below

REQUIRED FILE NAMING (CRITICAL)

Files are NOT automatically sorted.
All uploads go into a single secure folder.
Your file name is how we identify and organize your documents.

You MUST rename your files before uploading.

Use this exact format:

  • CATEGORY_LASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_DATE_DESCRIPTION

Examples:

  • A_Exhibits_None_Smith_John_2023-04-01_TranscriptPage.pdf
  • B_Not_Read_Doe_Jane_2022-10-07_JudgeStatement.png
  • E_FOC_Shutdown_Johnson_Mark_2024-01-15_MatterClosedEmail.pdf
WARNING:
Files that are not clearly named may not be usable or attributable.

WHAT TO UPLOAD (SIMPLE LIST)

A. Exhibits Submitted But “Not in Record”

Upload if:

  • You submitted exhibits to the court
  • The transcript, register, or record later says “EXHIBITS: NONE”

Examples: Exhibit list, transcript page, clerk record

B. Judge Did Not Read or Review Evidence

  • The judge stated they did not review exhibits
  • The judge ruled without reviewing evidence

Examples: Transcript, order language, sworn statement

C. Child Support / Income Imputation Issues

  • Your income was imputed unfairly
  • Real income evidence was ignored

Examples: Support order, pay stubs, job emails

D. Late Notice or Bench Warrant Issues

  • You received notice after a hearing occurred
  • A bench warrant issued without proper notice

E. Friend of the Court Refusal or “Shutdown”

This means:

  • FOC refused to enforce a valid order
  • FOC closed or dismissed your issue without a hearing
  • You were told the matter was “resolved,” “closed,” or “not enforceable” without judicial review

Examples: Emails, letters, portal messages stating enforcement was denied or closed
